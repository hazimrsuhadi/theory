About Me

Welcome to my professional space! I am a passionate music educator dedicated to deepening understanding and love for music, with a particular focus on music theory and classical chamber music. My mission is to make music education inclusive, democratized, and accessible, empowering learners of all backgrounds. Whether guiding students through harmonic analysis or the nuance of nonharmonic tones, I strive to make complex ideas feel intuitive and inspiring.

As a collaborative pianist, I bring my artistry to stages alongside singers, instrumentalists, and choirs — from intimate recitals to community concerts. My repertoire centers on classical masterpieces, while embracing contemporary works, especially those by Indonesian composers whose voices deserve wider recognition. I believe music flourishes through connection, and I am committed to sharing that experience with fellow musicians and audiences alike.
